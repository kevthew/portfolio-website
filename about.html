I am 33 years old and live in Colorado springs, Colorado. I am very professional and well mannered. I am never scared of rolling up my sleeves and putting in hard work. I am very dedicated to my craft and take any job I accept very seriously.

I have worn many hats from janitor to cook, although most of my time has been spent workin in the concrete field. The lack of growth oppurtunity and getting laid off in winter has triggered a desire for more, something new.

How I Got Into Coding

My introduction to coding came from an avid love of retro gaming. When i was in highschool i discoverd emulation of retro games on pc and loved the convienence but missed playing on the tv, which directed me to discover the Raspberry Pi. I purchased my first one about a year ago. From playing with the single board computer I gained knowledge of linux and more importantly I discovered Python. From that moment on I was obsessed. I studied tutorial after tutorial, watched video after video. I built a couple small projects from tutorials and built a tic-tac-toe game on my own. I knew then that this was what I wanted to do for a career and enrolled in lambda the next day.
